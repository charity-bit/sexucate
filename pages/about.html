Sexucate

Sexucate works along side experts in various fields to reduce stigmatization of topics concerning sexual and reproductive health and practices in young adolescents.

About

We provide information to young girls and boys on safe sex practices. We also connect victims who have been shunned by their families and friends for engaging in premarital sexual activities with experts such as doctors and psychologists.
